1、Do one thing at a time, and do well.
一次只做一件事，做到最好！
2、Keep on going never give up.
勇往直前， 决不放弃！
3、Whatever is worth doing is worth doing well.
任何值得做的事就值得把它做好！
4、Never say die.
永不气馁！
5、The best preparation for tomorrow is doing your best today.
对明天做好的准备就是今天做到最好！
6、Don't aim for success if you want it; just do what you love and believe in, and it will come naturally.
如果你想要成功，不要去追求成功；尽管做你自己热爱的事情并且相信它，成功自然到来。
